Gigs

David has recently had the pleasure of performing to The Worshipful Company of Goldsmiths in their magnificent hall! David has also recently played for Eversheds Sutherland with the Issy Owen Quartet, an exciting ensemble of young jazz professionals. He also plays regularly with the Salisbury Hot Club, with a residency at Rack and Tenter in the City.
As well as professional gigs, David has recently enjoyed Guildhall opportunities with the Jazz Orchestra and the Guildhall Festival Orchestra.
