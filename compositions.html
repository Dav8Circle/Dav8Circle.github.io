David Whyatt

Saxophonist

Compositions

Not only a performer, David is always happy to be engaged with composition work. He is currently writing a set of works to be performed for solo saxophone in the Priory Church at Lancaster, with the premiere due in Autumn 2023. David's compositional interests are varied and far reaching, from classically informed styles through to through-composed film scores.
